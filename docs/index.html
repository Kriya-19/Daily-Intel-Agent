🔥 Contrarian Take

Despite widespread optimism and massive investment in AI's rapid advancements, enterprise-level deployment is demonstrating significant, under-addressed operational and trustworthiness flaws. Many organizations are prematurely scaling AI agents without foundational controls.
A series of VentureBeat articles highlights critical 'AI gaps' in enterprises: difficulty measuring compute costs, high rates of agent security incidents with shared credentials, widespread 'hallucinations' (confident wrong answers) due to context issues, and unreliable internal evaluation metrics that fail in production. This indicates a focus on deployment speed over robust, secure, and reliable integration, suggesting the perceived progress might be more fragile than the headlines suggest.
