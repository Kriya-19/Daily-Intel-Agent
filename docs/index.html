🔥 Contrarian Take

While the headlines trumpet massive AI investments and advancements, the practical reality of enterprise AI adoption is far from mature, with significant gaps in security, context trust, and evaluation. Many 'AI agents' being deployed are still basic chatbots, masking deeper operational and trust issues that could hinder genuine transformative impact.
Multiple reports highlight that enterprises are buying AI infrastructure faster than they can measure costs, are experiencing frequent security incidents with agents sharing credentials, struggling with agents producing 'confidently wrong' information, and shipping agents to production that fail customers despite passing internal evaluations. This suggests a widespread disconnect between hype and effective deployment.
